Beef & Bean Burritos

  • 16oz Refried black beans
  • 1/2lb Beef
  • 1cup Shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2cup Salsa
  • 1/4tsp Garlic powder
  • 1/2tsp Dried oregano
  • 1 Lime
  • ~6 8"Tortillas
  1. Brown beef in a large skillet.
  2. Add in salsa.
  3. Add in beans.
  4. Add in oregano.
  5. Squeeze out a lime over the mixture.
  6. Divide the mixture upon tortillas to your liking, top with shredded cheese.
  7. Package and freeze them... or just eat them all.
