Cowboy Beans

  • 1lb Beef
  • 16oz Baked Beans
  • 16oz Chili
  • 1/2cup Onion
  • Shredded cheddar cheese as topping
  1. Brown the beef in a large skillet, drain the grease if you're a soft rider.
  2. Add in the baked beans.
  3. Add in the chili.
  4. Add in chopped up onion.
  5. Heat to your liking and top with shredded cheese.
  6. Chow down pardner'.
