Creamed Chicken

  • 10oz Cooked chicken
  • 11oz Cream of mushroom soup
  • 6oz Sliced mushrooms
  • 12oz Noodles
  • 1/2cup Onion
  • 2tsp Olive oil
  • 1cup Sour cream
  • Parsley for topping
  1. Cook your noodles in water, drain water when noodles are to your liking.
  2. Cook the onion in the oil in a large skillet until tender.
  3. Add in cooked chicken.
  4. Add in mushroom soup.
  5. Add in sliced mushrooms.
  6. Serve mixture over the cooked noodles.
  7. Top with parsley and LARP as a WW2 veteran eating your food: Sitting in your cheap house you bought with half a year's salary, reminisce of a war that you wish took your life. Ignore your kids watching tv, they'll live the easiest life the world has ever sustained, crashing the economy for generatons to come.
